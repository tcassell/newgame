Adventure-Seeker

  • High Concept: Explore the caverns! Avoid deadly bots and collect treasure!
  • Player Experience Goals: High tension
  • Genre: Top-down adventure
  • Game play: Use arrow keys to move around, avoid touching the bots, collect the stars to win
  • Prototype goal: Enemy "pathfinding" and collection system
  • Features: A guy with a spear avoiding bots!
  • Setting/Premise: You're a prehistoric guy exploring caverns with futuristic defense
  • Story: Imagination!
  • Target Audience: Casual gamers
  • Play time: 3-10 minutes
  • Strategies: Try to figure out when the bots change directions.
  • Prototype Assets: Music is original, sprites were obtained from http://gamefroot.com/adding-your-own-top-down-characters/
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: I borrowed the starstruck game and modified the character behaviour and sprites, and added new mechanics.
